Data engine

Local research store. MVP: in-memory + JSON snapshots; production: Postgres + TimescaleDB.

Pipelines

Snapshot & history pulls are wired to live IBKR endpoints. They run on demand from the Markets and Backtests pages — no scheduled ingest yet.
WebSocket streamer not yet started. Endpoint wss://api.ibkr.com/v1/api/ws?bearer_token=… available once connected.
